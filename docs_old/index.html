Hi. We are Fourhead.

And this is our project, PROJECT LERA, Luzon Electricity Reliability Analysis. The goal of Project LERA was to investigate the frequency of electric shortages experienced across different regions of Luzon in 2020-2023. It also compared the performance of public and private electric cooperatives in terms of the shortages. Data were primarily collected from the cooperatives' Facebook pages and websites.

Here's an overview of our project.

Motivation

Project LERA has the desire to improve the availability and reliability of electricity in Luzon. It aims to identify areas most affected by electricity shortages and raise awareness about electricity issues in Luzon .

Problem

The frequency of electric shortages are not well documented. There might be unreliable electricity access across different regions of Luzon and potential inefficiecy in how shortages are managed.

Solution

Project LERA analyzes data on electric shortages in Luzon from 2020 to 2023. This analysis focuses on how the type of electric cooperative affects the frequency of shortages, providing insights into the quality of service.

We mined the internet for data.

A data entry is made for every post, with these attributes:

ID

The post's unique identifier

Name of Cooperative

Name of post's source

Type of Cooperative

Either public or private

Region

Post's place of origin

Text

The actual 'raw' data
